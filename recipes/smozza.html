Smozza

Pizza S'more... you get the jist.

Ingredients

    2 Slices Pizza
  • 3-10 Marhsed Mallows
  • Chocolate (Optional)

Instructions

  1. roast two pieces of pizza over fire until dark and crispy
  2. While the Pizza is roasting toast your marshmallows to prefered toastiness
  3. Combine Pizza and marhmallows and add chocolate if desired
  4. Serve and Enjoy!
